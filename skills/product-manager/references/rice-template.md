# RICE prioritization

**RICE = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort.** Higher = do sooner.

- **Reach** — how many users/events per time period this affects (a real number).
- **Impact** — 3 = massive, 2 = high, 1 = medium, 0.5 = low, 0.25 = minimal.
- **Confidence** — 100% high, 80% medium, 50% low (how sure are the estimates?).
- **Effort** — person-weeks (or person-months) of total work.
