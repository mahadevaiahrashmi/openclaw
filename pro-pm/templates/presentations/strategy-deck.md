# Strategy Deck: [Product/Area Name]

<!-- Slide-by-slide outline for a product strategy presentation. 15–20 slides. -->

## Slide 1: Title
- **[Product Strategy: Year/Period]**
- [Presenter name and date]

## Slide 2: Executive Summary
- 3 bullets: where we are, where we're going, how we'll get there

## Slide 3: Mission & Vision
- Mission statement
- 3-year vision in one sentence

## Slide 4: Market Context
- Market size and growth rate
- Key trends shaping the landscape
- Our current market position

## Slide 5: Customer Insights
- Top 3 customer needs/pain points with supporting data
- Key persona summary

## Slide 6: Competitive Landscape
- 2x2 positioning map
- Our differentiation in one sentence

## Slide 7: Current Performance
- Key metrics dashboard: revenue, growth, retention, NPS
- Performance vs. targets

## Slide 8: Strategic Pillars
- 3–5 strategic themes for the year
- One sentence each on why they matter

## Slides 9–13: Deep Dive per Pillar
- One slide per strategic pillar:
  - Opportunity, approach, key initiatives, success metrics

## Slide 14: Roadmap Overview
- Visual timeline showing key milestones by quarter

## Slide 15: Resource Plan
- Team size and hiring plan
- Budget allocation across pillars

## Slide 16: Risks & Mitigations
- Top 3 risks with mitigation plans

## Slide 17: Success Metrics
- OKRs or key metrics dashboard with targets

## Slide 18: The Ask
- What you need from the audience: approval, resources, alignment

## Appendix Slides
- Detailed market data
- Competitive comparison tables
- Financial models

---
**Presenter Notes:** Target 30-minute presentation with 15 minutes for Q&A.
