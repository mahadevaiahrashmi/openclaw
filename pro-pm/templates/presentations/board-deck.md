# Board Deck: [Quarter Year]

<!-- Board of directors presentation outline. Product section: 8–10 slides. -->

## Slide 1: Product Section Title
- **Product Update: Q[X] [Year]**

## Slide 2: Executive Summary
- 3 bullets: biggest win, key metric, forward outlook
- Overall status: On Track / Needs Attention

## Slide 3: Key Metrics
- Dashboard: ARR, growth, retention, NPS, active users
- All metrics with targets and year-over-year comparison

## Slide 4: Product Highlights
- 2–3 major deliverables with customer impact
- Screenshot or visual for the most impactful one

## Slide 5: Customer Proof Point
- One compelling customer story or metric
- Customer logo and quote (if approved)

## Slide 6: Market & Competitive Context
- Competitive move or market shift worth noting
- Our positioning response

## Slide 7: Challenges
- 1–2 honest challenges with what we're doing about them
- Don't surprise the board — address known concerns

## Slide 8: Forward Outlook
- Next quarter priorities (3 bullets max)
- Key milestones and dates

## Slide 9: Strategic Question (Optional)
- One strategic topic where board input is valuable
- Frame as a question, not a presentation

## Slide 10: Appendix
- Detailed metric tables
- Feature roadmap detail
- Competitive comparison data

---
**Presenter Notes:**
- Keep to 15 minutes max. Boards want conversation, not monologue.
- Anticipate the top 3 questions and prepare answers.
- Send the deck 48 hours in advance per board protocol.
