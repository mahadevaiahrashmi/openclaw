## Risk Scoring Guide
- **Likelihood:** 1 (Rare) → 5 (Almost certain)
- **Impact:** 1 (Negligible) → 5 (Catastrophic)
- **Score:** Likelihood × Impact. ≥15 = Critical, 10-14 = High, 5-9 = Medium, <5 = Low

## Risk Categories
- **Technical:** Architecture, performance, security, integration
- **Market:** Competition, demand, timing, regulation
- **Team:** Capacity, skills, turnover, dependencies
- **Legal/Compliance:** Regulatory, contractual, privacy

## Escalation Criteria
- Score ≥ 15: Escalate to VP/executive immediately
- Score 10-14: Include in weekly leadership update
- Score 5-9: Monitor and mitigate within team
