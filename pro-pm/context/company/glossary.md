## Glossary

### ARR
**Definition:** Annual Recurring Revenue — the annualized value of active subscriptions.
**Context:** Primary revenue metric reported to board and investors.

### DACI
**Definition:** Driver, Approver, Contributors, Informed — a decision-making framework.
**Context:** Used for all major product decisions. PM is typically the Driver.

### DAU / WAU / MAU
**Definition:** Daily / Weekly / Monthly Active Users.
**Context:** Tracked in analytics; DAU/MAU ratio indicates stickiness.

### GTM
**Definition:** Go-to-Market — the strategy and process for launching products.
**Context:** GTM plans are owned by PMM with PM input.

### ICP
**Definition:** Ideal Customer Profile — the description of the perfect-fit customer.
**Context:** Used by Sales and Marketing to target efforts.

### JTBD
**Definition:** Jobs to Be Done — a framework for understanding customer needs.
**Context:** Used in discovery to frame problems as "jobs" customers hire the product for.

### NDR
**Definition:** Net Dollar Retention — revenue retained from existing customers including expansion.
**Context:** NDR >100% means existing customers are growing.

### NPS
**Definition:** Net Promoter Score — likelihood of customers recommending the product.
**Context:** Surveyed quarterly; tracked as company-level metric.

### PRD
**Definition:** Product Requirements Document — spec for what to build and why.
**Context:** Written by PM; reviewed by Eng Lead + Design before sprint planning.

### RICE
**Definition:** Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort — a prioritization scoring framework.
**Context:** Used in quarterly planning to rank feature candidates.

### TTV
**Definition:** Time to Value — how long until a new user gets value from the product.
**Context:** Key PLG metric; measured from signup to activation event.
