## How to Use

For each major initiative, copy the template below and fill in:
1. **DACI roles** — who Drives, Approves, Contributes, is Informed
2. **Alignment status** — where each stakeholder stands
3. **Influence paths** — how to reach unaligned stakeholders
4. **Coalition map** — who can help influence others
