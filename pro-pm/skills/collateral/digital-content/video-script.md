# Video Script

## Purpose
Creates a product video script with hook (first 5 seconds), problem framing, solution demonstration, benefits, and CTA. Includes timing marks, visual directions, and voiceover copy. Optimized for the platform (web, social, ads) and attention span of the target viewer.

## Auto-Trigger Patterns
- "Write a video script for [product/feature]"
- "Create a product demo video script"
- "I need a script for a [explainer/launch/testimonial] video"
- "Help me script a product video"

## Inputs

**Zero-setup:** Only the user prompt is required. If context files are empty, use `context/_defaults.md` and label assumptions. See `skills/_GLOBAL-BEHAVIOR.md`.

- **Required:** Product/feature, video type (explainer, demo, testimonial, ad), target length, key message, CTA
- **Optional:** Brand guidelines, existing footage, music preferences, platform (YouTube, social, website), talent
- **Reference files:** product-brief.md, product-launch-deck.md, landing-page-copy.md

## Process
1. **Define the format** — Explainer (concept), demo (product), testimonial (social proof), or ad (conversion).
2. **Nail the hook** — First 5 seconds determine if anyone watches. Shock, question, or visual surprise.
3. **Script to time** — 150 words per minute for voiceover. 90-second video = ~225 words max.
4. **Alternate visual and verbal** — Show and tell, don't just tell. Script visuals alongside audio.
5. **Build to CTA** — Every element builds toward one action.
6. **Edit ruthlessly** — Cut any scene that doesn't advance the story or the sale.

## Output Format
```
# Video Script: [Title]
**Duration:** [X seconds/minutes] | **Type:** [Explainer/Demo/Ad]
**Platform:** [YouTube/Social/Website] | **Aspect Ratio:** [16:9/1:1/9:16]

## [0:00-0:05] Hook
**Visual:** [What the viewer sees]
**Audio/VO:** "[Voiceover copy]"
**Text on screen:** [Any overlay text]

## [0:05-0:15] Problem
**Visual:** [Problem visualization]
**Audio/VO:** "[Problem statement]"

## [0:15-0:45] Solution
**Visual:** [Product in action — screen recording or animation]
**Audio/VO:** "[How the product solves the problem]"

## [0:45-1:05] Benefits
**Visual:** [Results, metrics, or happy users]
**Audio/VO:** "[Key benefits — 2-3 specific outcomes]"

## [1:05-1:15] Social Proof
**Visual:** [Customer logos, quote, or testimonial clip]
**Audio/VO:** "[Credibility builder]"

## [1:15-1:25] CTA
**Visual:** [Product screen with CTA overlay]
**Audio/VO:** "[Clear call to action]"
**Text on screen:** [CTA + URL]

## Production Notes
- **Music:** [Mood, tempo, reference track]
- **Pacing:** [Fast/measured/dramatic]
- **Talent:** [Voiceover, on-camera, or animated]
- **B-roll needs:** [Specific shots or animations needed]
```

## Quality Standards
- The hook stops a scroll within 3 seconds—test by watching with sound off.
- Product footage shows real product, not mockups (unless pre-launch).
- CTA is specific and time-bound when possible.
- **Anti-patterns:** Slow opening, feature list narration, no hook, CTA buried at the end of a long video, poor audio quality.

## Audience
Varies by platform. YouTube: longer attention span, searching for solutions. Social: scrolling, sound-off, need visual hooks. Website: already interested, want confirmation. Match video style to the viewer's mindset and platform norms.

## Formatting Guidelines
- Two-column format: visual (left) and audio (right).
- Timing marks every 5-10 seconds.
- Indicate text overlays, transitions, and music cues.
- Platform-specific aspect ratios and length limits noted.

## Example
**[0:00-0:05] Hook:** Visual: Split screen—left side shows a person juggling 5 browser tabs frantically, right side shows one clean search bar. VO: "What if finding anything at work took two seconds instead of twenty minutes?" Text: "Stop searching. Start finding."
