# Social Media — LinkedIn

## Purpose
Creates LinkedIn content in multiple formats—personal posts, company updates, carousel outlines, and articles—with a professional, insight-led tone designed to drive engagement and establish thought leadership.

## Auto-Trigger Patterns
- "Write a LinkedIn post about [topic]"
- "Create LinkedIn content for [announcement/insight]"
- "I need LinkedIn posts for [launch/campaign]"
- "Help me write a professional social post"

## Inputs

**Zero-setup:** Only the user prompt is required. If context files are empty, use `context/_defaults.md` and label assumptions. See `skills/_GLOBAL-BEHAVIOR.md`.

- **Required:** Topic or announcement, key message, target audience, CTA (if any)
- **Optional:** Supporting data, personal angle, company context, related content to link, hashtags
- **Reference files:** product-brief.md, blog-post-thought-leadership.md, blog-post-announcement.md

## Process
1. **Choose the format** — Personal narrative, data insight, carousel, company announcement, or article.
2. **Hook in line 1** — The first 1-2 lines appear before "see more." They must compel a click.
3. **Deliver value** — Teach something, share an insight, or tell a story. Not a pitch.
4. **End with engagement** — Question, poll, or strong opinion that invites response.
5. **Optimize formatting** — Short lines, white space, emojis sparingly, hashtags at bottom.

## Output Format
```
# LinkedIn Content: [Topic]

## Personal Post (Insight/Story)
[Hook line — surprising stat, bold statement, or story opener]

[2-3 short paragraphs developing the insight]

[Key takeaway or lesson — formatted for impact]

[Engagement question or CTA]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3

---

## Company Post (Announcement)
[Company voice announcement. What + why it matters.]

[Key benefits or results. Tagged people if relevant.]

[CTA: Link to blog/product/event]

---

## Carousel Outline (5-8 slides)
Slide 1: [Hook/title]
Slide 2: [Problem or context]
Slide 3-6: [Key points, one per slide]
Slide 7: [Summary/takeaway]
Slide 8: [CTA + follow prompt]

---

## Article Format (if longer form needed)
[Title, subtitle, 500-800 word article outline]
```

## Quality Standards
- The hook line would make a scrolling professional stop and click "see more."
- Content provides value independent of any product pitch.
- Personal posts use authentic voice; company posts use brand voice.
- **Anti-patterns:** Humble brags, "thrilled to announce" openings, pure self-promotion, no engagement hook, wall of text.

## Audience
LinkedIn professionals in your industry—potential customers, partners, recruits, and industry peers. They scroll quickly, engage with value, and share content that makes them look thoughtful.

## Formatting Guidelines
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences). Line breaks between paragraphs.
- Hook in first 2 lines (visible before "see more").
- 3-5 hashtags maximum, placed at the end.
- Tag people and companies when relevant (increases reach).

## Example
**Personal Post:** "I used to think product-market fit was binary. You either have it or you don't.\n\nAfter 3 products and 8 years, I've realized it's more like a radio dial.\n\nYou're always tuning. The signal gets stronger, then weaker, then stronger again.\n\nHere's what changes the signal strength:\n\n→ Market shifts (new regulations, new competitors)\n→ Customer evolution (their needs change as they grow)\n→ Your own growth (new segments, new use cases)\n\nThe companies that win aren't the ones who 'find' PMF.\n\nThey're the ones who keep tuning.\n\nWhat's shifting your signal right now?"
